Beating the Game and Beyond

Mid-Game (Nether Fortress, Potions, Pearls)

Once iron gear and basic supplies are ready, advance to mid‑game objectives and start working toward beating the game.

  • Locate a Nether Fortress: In the Nether, roam at Y‑level ~64 and look for nether brick bridges. Nether Fortresses can spawn anywhere, but exploring Basalt Deltas, Warped Forests, and Nether Wastes biomes is effective. Use torches to light your path and block lava flows with a bucket.
  • Collect Blaze rods: Inside the fortress, find and kill Blazes at spawners. Blaze rods drop (about one rod per two Blazes). You need 4 rods (to make 12 blaze powder) for brewing and Eyes of Ender, plus extras for fuel. Build a safe cobblestone trap around the spawner to farm rods.
  • Brew crucial potions: Use a Brewing Stand and Nether wart to make an Awkward Potion base. Key potions: Strength (adds melee damage against the dragon), Regeneration/Healing, and Slow Falling. Brewing Strength II (add blaze powder + glowstone) and Regeneration II (add glowstone) is ideal. Slow Falling requires Phantom Membranes (brew Awkward + Phantom Membrane). To get membranes, avoid sleeping until Phantoms spawn at night and kill them (they drop membranes).
  • Obtain Ender Pearls: Ender Pearls are needed for Eyes of Ender and in‑fight maneuvers. Efficient methods:
    • Kill Endermen: At night in the Overworld or in Nether Warped Forests, kill Endermen for pearls (50% drop chance, up to 4 with Looting III). The Warped Forest biome has high Enderman spawn rates, making it ideal.
    • Piglin Bartering: In the Nether, trade gold ingots with Piglins (~4.3% chance to get 3–4 pearls per ingot). Gather gold in Bastion Remnants and barter often—no fighting Endermen required.
    • Cleric Trading (optional): Expert Cleric villagers can sell pearls (2⁄3 chance) for 5 emeralds each.
  • Gather gold and obsidian: Fortresses and Bastions contain gold blocks and obsidian in chests. Mine these for Piglin trades and portal construction.
  • Progress your gear: Aim for diamond (or Netherite) armor and tools, craft a bow or crossbow, and collect ≥30 XP levels from Blazes or other mobs.

Preparing for The End

  • Strong Armor and Enchants: Full diamond or Netherite set with Protection IV and Feather Falling IV on boots. Add Unbreaking III and Mending via anvil or trades.
  • Weapons and Tools: Diamond/Netherite sword (Sharpness V) and bow (Power V + Infinity) or crossbow (Multishot/Piercing). Pack a pickaxe, too.
  • Food and Healing: High‑saturation food and Golden Apples (or Enchanted Golden Apples, if available).
  • Potions: Carry Slow Falling, Strength, and Regeneration/Healing (drink or splash) potions.
  • Inventory Layout: Sword, bow, pickaxe, golden apples, water bucket, slow-fall potion, ender pearls, blocks.
  • Enchanting Tricks: Enchant books at a 15‑bookshelf table, then apply via anvil. Use grindstone to strip bad enchants (recovers some XP).

Locating and Activating the End Portal

  • Craft Eyes of Ender: At least 15 total (12 for the portal + extras for breakage).
  • Finding the Stronghold: Throw an Eye of Ender, follow its flight, and repeat until it descends. Plot multiple lines or use two players to triangulate quickly.
  • Navigating the Stronghold: Cautiously explore corridors, listen for silverfish, kill the spawner, and block off hazards.
  • Activating the Portal: Insert Eyes into each empty frame; portal will activate with a roar.
  • Final Prep: Arrange your hotbar (sword, bow, golden apples, potions, blocks), then jump in.

Defeating the Ender Dragon

  • Initial Landing: Immediately pearl or build to the main island. Press F3+B (Java) to see Enderman hitboxes.
  • Take Out End Crystals: The dragon heals from crystals on obsidian pillars. Safe methods:
    • Shoot from ground: Bow, snowballs, or eggs break them.
    • Climb towers: Pillar up or pearl to the top and break cages.
    • Water elevator: Place water at the base and swim up (carry a bucket).
  • Avoid Endermen: Place water around you or wear a carved pumpkin to keep them passive.
  • Knockback Safety: Use a water-bucket MLG or slow-falling potion to negate knock-up damage.
  • Dealing Damage:
    • Ranged: Use Power V/Infinity bow or spectral arrows.
    • Melee: Strike the head when the dragon perches on the portal.
    • Beds: Explode beds inside the portal ring for huge damage.
    • End Crystals: Place and shoot your own crystals for extra explosion damage.
  • Staying Alive: Keep moving, eat golden apples, drink regen, and use pearls to teleport back if knocked away.
  • Final Blow: Continue attacking until the dragon dies. Enjoy the explosion and purple beams.

After the Dragon

  • Collect the Dragon Egg: Break the block under it with a torch two blocks down so it drops onto the torch.
  • Exit Portal: Jump in to see the End Poem and credits.
  • End Gateways & End Cities: Throw a pearl into a gateway to reach outer islands. Loot Elytra, shulker boxes, and chorus fruit.
  • Keep Exploring: Build farms, projects, or simply fly with Elytra and fireworks across the End.

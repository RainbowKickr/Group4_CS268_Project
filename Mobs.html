Mobs spawn everywhere and in every dimension. There are three type of mobs: passive, neutral and hostile.

Passive mobs will not attack you back under any circemstance
Neutral mobs will spawn as passive, but under certain conditions they will attack you
Hostile mobs will always try to attack when you are in range for them to see you

Enderman

 20 hearts Neutral

Passive when player wears carved pumpkin

 Everywhere and in all dimensions barring the Deep Dark in the Overworld and anywhere in the Nether with light level above 7 excluding Nether Wastes, Soul Sand Valley and Warped Forest Takes damage when touching water, including when it rains. Will become hostile if shot at or looked in the eyes by a player

Can drop enderpearls when killed

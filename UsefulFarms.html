Step 1: Automatic Wheat Farm

The automatic wheat farm is an essential redstone contraption that allows players to easily harvest wheat without manual effort. To build one, you'll need dispensers, water buckets, and redstone components like observers and pistons. The design involves placing dispensers with water buckets that will flood and harvest the crops when activated. A redstone clock or observer can trigger the dispensers at regular intervals, ensuring the wheat is automatically watered and ready to be harvested. This farm can also be adapted for other crops like carrots or potatoes.

Step 2: Automatic Tree Farm

An automatic tree farm utilizes bone meal and pistons to generate and harvest trees. The tree grows with the help of bone meal and then pistons push the tree into a collection area. A hopper system underneath will gather the wood and saplings, which are then stored in chests. For this farm, you'll need to plant saplings and ensure the bone meal dispenser is constantly supplied with bones. With a redstone clock, the pistons are triggered, allowing for continuous wood production. The farm can be customized for different tree types like oak, spruce, or jungle trees.

Step 3: Automatic Mob Farm

An automatic mob farm is useful for gathering resources like string, bones, and gunpowder. This redstone farm uses a combination of dark spawning platforms, water currents, and a drop chute to kill mobs. Mobs spawn in dark areas and are funneled into a drop chute where they fall to their death. Once they die, a hopper system collects the drops. Step 4: Automatic Sugar Cane Farm

Sugar cane farms are incredibly useful for obtaining paper and crafting books. To create an automatic sugar cane farm, place sugar cane near water and use pistons to harvest them. When the sugar cane reaches a certain height, observers will detect the growth and trigger pistons to break the crop. The broken sugar cane will then be pushed into a hopper system that leads to chests for storage. This farm can be expanded vertically for more efficiency, allowing you to harvest a large quantity of sugar cane in a short amount of time.

Step 5: Automatic Villager Trading Hall

For a more advanced farm, the automatic villager trading hall is essential for acquiring rare items from villagers. This farm uses a redstone-powered system to move villagers between trading stations, where they can be locked into trades with items like enchanted books, emeralds, and more. The system is set up with minecarts, dispensers, and powered rails to move the villagers to the correct station. You can easily control the trades and automate the collection of goods. By combining a villager breeder and trading hall, you can generate a steady stream of resources from the villagers.
